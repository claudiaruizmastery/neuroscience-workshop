Your Guide on the Journey

Claudia Ruiz

Official Dr Joe Dispenza NCS Consultant

My purpose is to guide you in mastering the practical application of neuroscience to create profound and lasting change in your life.

While I am an Industrial Engineer by training, my heart has always been drawn to understanding how systems work—especially the most powerful one of all: the human mind and body. What began as a search for efficiency evolved into a passion for transformation. Today, I use that same systems-thinking approach to guide others through a practical, empowering path to real and lasting change.

My expertise has been forged through more than a decade of dedicated, hands-on work under the direct mentorship of Dr. Joe Dispenza. My journey began in 2011 as part of the original group of certified trainers, which gave me a deep understanding of how to teach this work effectively from the very beginning.

From there, my path unfolded. I served for over eight years on the brain-mapping team—a role acknowledged in "Becoming Supernatural"—where I didn't just observe the science; I witnessed thousands of people's lives changing in real-time. I've also served as a Team Leader, supporting participants directly through their most challenging and triumphant moments. Today, I continue this service at Dr. Joe's advanced workshops as a "walking Q&A," a trusted resource on the floor there to offer the insight needed to help someone break through.

Each of these roles was more than a job; it was a form of mentorship, a different way of learning directly from Dr. Joe and from the collective experience of thousands of participants.

This entire journey has shaped my ability to be the bridge for you. My role is to answer your questions with a depth that comes from a decade of experience, simplify the complex, and create a supportive space where you can move from theory to true embodiment. It is this combination of deep experience and genuine connection that helps people turn life's challenges into catalysts for their next evolution.

Workshop Details & Registration

Your 6-Week Journey to Embodied Transformation

This program is meticulously designed to guide you from intellectual understanding to true embodiment. Each phase builds upon the last, ensuring you have the structure and support needed to create lasting change.

Phase 1: The Foundation (Your Private 1:1 Session)

We begin with a private and confidential 1:1 preparatory call. This essential first step allows us to understand your unique goals, answer your initial questions, and set a clear, personal intention for your transformation. This ensures you arrive at the workshop ready to gain the maximum benefit.

Phase 2: The Immersion (The 2-Day Online Intensive)

This is where the core learning happens. Over two full days, you will dive deep into the models, science, and practical tools of Dr. Joe's "Change Your Mind... Create New Results" program. Through a combination of teaching, group activities, and real-time practice, you will move from thinking about the work to truly experiencing it.

Phase 3: The Integration (6 Weeks of Guided Support)

True mastery is built through daily practice. Following the intensive, you will receive six weeks of dedicated support to help you integrate these new skills into your life. This includes:

  • Four Live Group Follow-up Calls: These sessions provide a space to ask questions, share progress, and deepen your understanding as you navigate real-world challenges and successes.
  • Direct Group Support via Telegram: You'll have direct access to Claudia and the group for ongoing questions, encouragement, and shared insights throughout the six weeks.
  • Essential Materials: You will also receive the official workbook, a 30-day journal, and all of Dr. Joe's audio lectures and meditations to support your practice.
